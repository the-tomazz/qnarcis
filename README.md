# QNarcIS

QGIS plugin for the Slovenian Nature Information System - NarcIS  
QGIS-vtičnik za informacijski sistem za naravo - NarcIS

## Slovensko

Vtičnik QNarcIS omogoča delo s prostorskimi podatki v Sloveniji in zagotavlja neposreden dostop do biodiverzitetnih, naravovarstvenih in podpornih podatkov ter njihovi analizi v orodju QGIS, ki je standard pri uporabi GIS v slovenskem varstvu narave. Njegova povezanost z informacijskim sistemom za naravo - NarcIS podpira napredno izvajanje prostorskih poizvedb in preprost dostop do specifičnih podatkovnih slojev.

QNarcIS omogoča na enem mestu dostop do različnih virov več sto javno dostopnih slojev prostorskih podatkov. S funkcionalnostjo "Pošlji poligon" je podprto izvajanje prostorskih poizvedb neposredno v portalu NarcIS. Vtičnik tako pomembno prispeva k podpori strokovnemu odločanju in izvajanju nalog na področjih varstva narave, umeščanja v prostor in preučevanja biodiverzitete.

Vtičnik vsebuje tudi navodila za uporabo in objave o aktualnih novostih vtičnika iz GIS-foruma v portalu NarcIS. Razvit je bil v okviru projekta LIFE NarcIS (https://narcis.gov.si), dalje se razvija in nadgrajuje v okviru projekta SLO4D (https://www.gov.si/zbirke/projekti-in-programi/zeleni-slovenski-lokacijski-okvir-slo4d). Za Agencijo Republike Slovenije za okolje - ARSO, ki je skrbnik vtičnika, ga je izdelal Spatial mind (https://spatialmind.org).

Vtičnik je mogoče prilagoditi tudi za delo v tujini.

## English

The QNarcIS plugin enables working with spatial data in Slovenia and provides direct access to biodiversity, nature conservation, and supplementary spatial data, as well as their analysis within QGIS, which is the standard GIS tool used in Slovenian nature conservation. Its integration with the Slovenian Nature Information System - NarcIS supports advanced spatial analyses and easy access to specific data layers.

QNarcIS provides centralized access to various sources of hundreds of publicly available spatial data layers. Through the "Send polygon" functionality, it enables spatial queries to be executed directly within the NarcIS portal. The plugin thus makes a significant contribution to supporting expert decision-making and the implementation of tasks in nature conservation, spatial planning, and biodiversity research in Slovenia.

The plugin also includes user instructions and announcements of current updates from the GIS forum in the NarcIS portal. QNarcIS was developed within the framework of the LIFE NarcIS project (https://narcis.gov.si) and continues to be developed and upgraded as part of the SLO4D project (https://www.gov.si/zbirke/projekti-in-programi/zeleni-slovenski-lokacijski-okvir-slo4d). It was created for the Slovenian Environment Agency - ARSO, which maintains the plugin, by Spatial mind (https://spatialmind.org).

The plugin can also be adapted for use outside Slovenia.
